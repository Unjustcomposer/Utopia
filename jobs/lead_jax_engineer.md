# Lead JAX Engineer

## About NexusAI
NexusAI is building the first fully differentiable macroeconomic simulator, bypassing traditional discrete Agent-Based Models.

## Role
We are seeking a Lead JAX Engineer to architect and optimize our differentiable economic engine, ensuring it scales to millions of continuous entities.

## Responsibilities
- Architect the core simulation engine using JAX.
- Implement and optimize Ghosting Masks and continuous masking techniques.
- Scale simulations using XLA and TPU/GPU clusters.

## Requirements
- Extensive experience with JAX, XLA, and automatic differentiation.
- Strong background in high-performance computing or machine learning systems.
- Proficiency in Python.

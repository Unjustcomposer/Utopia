# Chief Economist

## About NexusAI
NexusAI is building a new paradigm for policy optimization using Differentiable Macroeconomic Graph Engines. We simulate the economy as a continuous computational graph.

## Role
We are seeking a Chief Economist to guide our macroeconomic modeling, calibrate our differentiable simulators to real-world data, and define the objective functions for policy optimization.

## Responsibilities
- Design and validate macroeconomic models.
- Work closely with JAX engineers to translate economic theories into differentiable graphs.
- Author research papers and policy briefs.

## Requirements
- PhD in Economics or related field.
- Deep understanding of Agent-Based Models (ABMs) and macroeconomic policy.
- Experience with quantitative modeling and calibration.
